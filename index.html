Wei Shi

施伟 (shī wěi)

LLM Algorithm Engineer

Email

Wei Shi received his Ph.D. Magna Cum Laude in December 2020 and joined DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group as a Senior Algorithm Engineer in 2021. He was a Ph.D. candidate and junior researcher working on Natural Language Processing under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Vera Demberg at The Department of Language Science and Technology and Collaborative Research Center SFB-1102 of Saarland University in Germany.

2024-01: Wei Shi has left MiniMax Inc, but is still working on LLMs.
2022-06: Wei Shi has left Alibaba and joined MiniMax Inc., a startup company focusing on LLM and Multimodality models.
