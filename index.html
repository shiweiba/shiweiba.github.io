Wei Shi   施伟(shī wěi)

Ph.D Candidate / Junior Researcher


Wei Shi is a Ph.D Candidate and Junior Researcher working on Natural Language Processing, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Vera Demberg, at The Department of Language Science and Technology and Collaborative Research Center SFB-1102 of Saarland University in Germany. Right now he is mostly working on Discourse Relation Parsing, Neural Language Generation and Text Classification. He is also interested in using deep learning methods to solve NLP related tasks.
