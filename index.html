Wei Shi (施伟)

Ph.D Candidate / Junior Researcher

Wei Shi is a Ph.D Candidate and Junior Researcher working on Natural Language Processing, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Vera Demberg, at Department of Computational Linguistics & Phonetics. and Collaborative Research Center SFB-1102 of Saarland University in Germany. Right now he is mostly working on Discourse Relation Parsing and Text Classification. He is also interested in using deep learning methods to solve NLP related tasks.
